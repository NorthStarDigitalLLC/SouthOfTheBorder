From a Backyard Kitchen to the Bayou

Hot Headz started with a dream and a ghost pepper. Chef Marie-Louise infused her grandmother's Cajun recipes with Louisiana fire, creating a menu that pays homage to tradition while turning up the heat.

Every dish is made from scratch — from our roux to our remoulade. Come taste the South like never before.
