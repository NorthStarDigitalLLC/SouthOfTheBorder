OUR STORY

From a backyard kitchen to the bayou.

Hot Headz started with a dream and a ghost pepper. Chef Marie-Louise infused her grandmother's recipes with Louisiana fire, creating a menu that pays homage to tradition while turning up the heat. Every gumbo, every wing, every po'boy carries the soul of the South and a kick you won't forget. Come join us – but bring a glass of sweet tea.
