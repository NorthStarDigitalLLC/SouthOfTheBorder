Our Story

Hot Headz started in a tiny kitchen on Spice Alley, where Chef Marie-Louise blended her grandmother's Cajun recipes with a bold, modern twist. Every dish is made from scratch — from our roux to our remoulade — and we never hold back on the heat.

We’re a family‑owned spot that believes in big flavors, generous portions, and a warm welcome. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, come taste the South like never before.
