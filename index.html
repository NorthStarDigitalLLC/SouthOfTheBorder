FAQ

Common questions

Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Family Land Tree Service is fully licensed by the Louisiana State Licensing Board For Contractors (License #67905) and registered with the Louisiana Secretary of State (License #36054444K) . We carry full liability insurance for your protection.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all tree services. Contact us to schedule an appointment – we'll assess your trees and provide a detailed quote.
Do you handle emergency storm damage?
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for storm-damaged trees and hazardous situations. Call our emergency line for immediate response.
How long have you been in business?
Family Land Tree Service has been serving the Deridder community since November 16, 2005 – over 18 years of trusted, family-owned service .
Do you clean up after the job?
Absolutely. Complete cleanup is included with every job. We rake, haul away all debris, and leave your property spotless. It's our guarantee.
